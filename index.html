Welcome to Stadium Piece

In between Western Washington University's Academic West building and Communications Facility, Bruce Nauman's Stadium Piece can be found. The artist created this installation at the "front steps" of WWU to invite the viewer to take a step or to become a performer. Many of us may often walk past this installation without a second thought.
